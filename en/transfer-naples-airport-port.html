Naples Airport to Port Transfer

Direct transfer service between Naples Capodichino Airport and Naples Beverello port. Ideal for those who need to reach Ischia quickly with luggage support.

How the transfer works

Starting point: Naples Capodichino Airport (NAP)

Destination: Naples Beverello port, then ferry to Ischia

On-time pickup, luggage support, ferry schedule coordination.

FAQ

  • Is the transfer private? Yes, exclusive service for the customer.
  • Can I book the ferry too? Yes, full assistance to Ischia.
  • How long does the transfer take? About 25 minutes from airport to port.
