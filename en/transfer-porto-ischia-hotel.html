Final leg in Ischia

Ischia port to hotel transfer with experience since 2006

This page focuses on the final leg of the journey: you arrive at Ischia Porto or Casamicciola and continue directly to the hotel, resort or villa. It is designed for travelers who already have the trip to the island organized and want to avoid improvised taxis, waiting at the dock or uncertainty on arrival at the property.

How the transfer works

  • Pickup at Ischia Porto or Casamicciola terminal.
  • Clear meeting point instructions on arrival.
  • Luggage support during the main handover steps.
  • Direct drop-off at hotel, resort or villa.

Quick answers

  • How do I get from Ischia port to my hotel? If you book in advance, your driver will be there on arrival and take you straight to the hotel.
  • Can I book for a group? Yes, private cars, minivans and group solutions are available with luggage.
  • Does the service also cover Casamicciola? Yes, pickups are available from both Ischia Porto and Casamicciola.
  • Does the service help with luggage? Yes, practical support is provided during the main stages of the route.
