FAQ

Frequently asked questions from travelers

Can I book a transfer from Ischia Porto to my hotel?

Yes, the service includes pickup at Ischia Porto, Casamicciola and Forio with direct arrival at your hotel.

Does the service include luggage assistance?

Yes, luggage support is included during loading and unloading.

Is the transfer available in the evening too?

Yes, we coordinate the service according to ferry arrival times, including evening arrivals.

What happens after your request

  1. 1. We receive your request — your message reaches our operations team directly.
  2. 2. We contact you with availability and details — fast reply with timing, the pickup point at the port and directions to reach your hotel.
  3. 3. You confirm the transfer — you receive an operational summary and can plan with peace of mind.
