FAQ Procida Transfer

  • How do I get to Procida from Naples? Transfer from Naples airport or port to Pozzuoli port or Naples Beverello, then ferry to Procida with pickup at Marina Grande.
  • Is the airport-to-port transfer included? Yes, we manage the whole route from Capodichino to the boarding points for Procida.
  • How long is the transfer from Naples to Procida? The road section takes about 20 to 40 minutes; the ferry adds another 35 to 45 minutes depending on the chosen departure.
