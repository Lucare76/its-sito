Useful FAQ

Frequently asked questions before booking

Quick answers to the most common questions before choosing a transfer between Naples, the port and Ischia.

How do I get from Naples Airport to Ischia?

We arrange the transfer from Capodichino or Naples Centrale to Beverello and, if needed, the continuation to Ischia port or your hotel.

Is there a private transfer from Naples airport to the ferry port?

Yes. We take you from the airport to the ferry terminal with luggage support and practical instructions for the right pier.

Can I book a transfer to my hotel in Ischia?

Yes, we arrange private transfers from Ischia Porto, Casamicciola and Forio to hotels, resorts, apartments and villas, including bulky luggage or strollers.
