Useful FAQ

Frequently asked questions before booking

Simple answers to the most common questions about Naples to Ischia transfer, the port and hotel arrival.

How do I get from Naples Airport to Ischia?

We can arrange the transfer from Naples Capodichino to Naples Beverello and, if needed, the continuation on to Ischia as well.

Is there a private transfer from Naples airport to the ferry port?

Yes. We take you from the airport to the ferry terminal, with coordinated timing and luggage support.

Can I book a transfer to my hotel in Ischia?

Yes, we arrange private transfers from Ischia Porto, Casamicciola and Forio to hotels, resorts, apartments and villas.
