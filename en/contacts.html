Useful questions before booking

  • How do I get to Ischia from Naples? We can arrange the transfer from the airport or train station to the ferry port and, if needed, coordinate the journey all the way to your hotel in Ischia.
  • Do you provide Naples airport to port transfer? Yes, with pickup at Capodichino, luggage support and timing coordinated with departure from Beverello.
  • How long is the airport-to-port transfer? It usually takes 20-35 minutes, depending on traffic and the time of day.

For a faster reply, include departure point, destination, passenger count, date and estimated arrival time in your request.
