Complete service for travelers arriving by train in Naples and continuing to Ischia: we coordinate station pickup, port transfer, ferry connection and final hotel drop-off. This page is focused on the full route up to the accommodation; if you only need the first segment toward the ferry port, see the Naples train station transfer page. This route is ideal for families, evening arrivals, groups and guests traveling with multiple suitcases.

How the service works

  • Arrival at train station: scheduled pickup at Naples Centrale with direct contact.
  • Transfer to the port: fast route to ferry departure point with luggage support.
  • Ferry assistance: practical guidance and timing coordination.
  • Arrival in Ischia and hotel transfer: final transfer to your accommodation.

Quick answers

  • How do I get to Ischia from Naples train station? With station pickup, transfer to the port and coordinated ferry connection to Ischia.
  • Does the transfer include the port? Yes, station-to-port transfer is included in the service flow.
  • How long does the transfer take? Station-to-port is usually 15-30 minutes, while total route depends on ferry schedule and hotel area.
  • Is luggage assistance included? Yes, practical luggage support is included during the main transfer steps.
